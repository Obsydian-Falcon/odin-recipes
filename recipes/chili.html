Six Ingredient Chili

Here's an easy chili recipe that anybody can enjoy! No matter the weather or the time of year, this recipe is sure to be delicious and warming.

This recipe calls for six ingredients, ground beef, cheese, kidney beans, sour cream, beef stock, and finally, some chives for color.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Beef Stock
  • Cheese (of your choice)
  • Chives
  • Kidney Beans
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Remove the ground beef from its packaging and place it into a pan.
  2. Set the stove to medium-heat and spice the beef as you'd like.
  3. Transfer the beef to a stockpot and pour in the beef stock.
  4. Bring the affair to a simmer and add in the beans.
  5. Let sit at a low simmer for 1 hour.
  6. After one hour, take thepot off and add the cheese and chives.
  7. Serve with Sour Cream and enjoy!
