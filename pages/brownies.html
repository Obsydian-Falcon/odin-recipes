Wonderous Brownies

Wonderous brownies. Why wonderous? Because they taste great and will get you to a great place. This easy is simple, delicious, and will be sure to not only fulfill your hunger needs, but other social needs as well.

Equipment

  • 8x8 Baking Pan
  • Mixing Bowls
  • Handmixer
  • Spatula

Ingredients

  • 3/4 Cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 Cup Cannabutter
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â° F. Grease an 8"x8" metal baking pan and set it aside.
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, mix the dry ingredients. Sift the flour and the cocoa powder to remove any lumps, then add the salt, followed by the baking powder. Set aside.
  3. In a separate medium-sized bowl, cream the cannabutter, granulated white, and brown sugar together with a hand mixer.
  4. Add the eggs and vanilla to the creamed sugar and beat on medium speed for one minute (the mixture's color should lighten a bit).
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and beat on low speed until everything is well incorporated.
  6. Transfer the batter to the reserved greased baking pan. Lightly tap the pan to even out the batter.
  7. If you are adding and any optional toppings, like sea salt, add them now.
  8. Place the pan in the oven to bake for 25-30 minutes. The time will depend on your oven and your brownie consistency preferences. Test the finished brownies with a toothpick* (see notes below).
  9. If you can wait, allow the brownies to cool for 30 minutes before cutting into squares. Enjoy and repeat!
